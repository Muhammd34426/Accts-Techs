Got Questions?

We Have Answers

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly and we'll be happy to help.

What services does Accts & Techs offer?
We offer a full range of professional business services including Bookkeeping, Payroll Processing, Tax & Accounting, Company Formations, IT Recruitment, Contracting Solutions and Outsourcing. All services are tailored to the needs of small businesses across Ireland.
Do you offer a free consultation?
Yes — your first consultation is completely free of charge. We encourage all new and prospective clients to schedule a call or meeting so we can understand your business needs and recommend the most suitable services and payment plan.
Can you handle payroll for my employees?
Absolutely. We provide accurate, compliant payroll processing on a monthly, quarterly or annual basis. We ensure your team is always paid correctly and on time, and we handle all Revenue Commissioners submissions on your behalf.
Do I need to be based in Kildare to use your services?
No — we service clients across Dublin and all surrounding counties. Our services are available both in-house and remotely, so location is never a barrier. Many of our clients avail of our fully remote bookkeeping and payroll service.
What are your payment options?
We offer flexible payment plans to suit every budget. We discuss options during your free first consultation, and we can structure monthly or per-service payment arrangements depending on your preferences and requirements.
Can you help me set up a new company in Ireland?
Yes. Our Company Formations service handles the entire registration process on your behalf — from filing with the Companies Registration Office to setting up your business for VAT and tax obligations. We make starting your business as straightforward as possible.
Are your bookkeepers fully qualified?
Yes — all our bookkeepers are fully qualified professionals. We also provide backup cover in the event of staff absences, ensuring that your business continuity is never disrupted. Our team is experienced across multiple accounting software platforms.
How do I get started with Accts & Techs?
Simply get in touch with us by phone at 045 254 212 or email info@acctechs.ie — or complete the free consultation form on our website. One of our team will contact you promptly to arrange an initial consultation at no charge.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is always happy to help. Call us, email us or book your free consultation today.
