Audit Exemption Advisory — Current Thresholds

Following recent increases in audit exemption thresholds in Ireland, many companies now qualify for audit exemption status. A company may generally qualify where, in the current and preceding financial year, it satisfies at least two of the following criteria:

  • Annual turnover not exceeding €12 million
  • Gross assets not exceeding €6 million
  • Average number of employees not exceeding 50

Our team will assess your company's eligibility and advise on the most appropriate compliance approach, including preparing and filing all necessary documentation to claim audit exemption status. We also advise on matters that may impact eligibility, such as late filing of annual returns with the CRO, which can result in temporary loss of audit exemption entitlement.
